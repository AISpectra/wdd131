Visual Hierarchy & Contrast

The goal of visual hierarchy is to organize elements in a way that clearly indicates importance. Headings are larger and bolder, content is structured to flow from most important to least. Good contrast ensures that text is readable and elements stand out.

This is a callout section designed to draw attention to key ideas. But we must ensure it's accessible to everyone.

  • Use headings and font size to show importance
  • Keep layouts consistent
  • Test your design's contrast and readability

Design for everyone. Make it accessible and beautiful.
