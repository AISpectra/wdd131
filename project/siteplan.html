Website Planning Document

Site Name

Spectra – Emotional Support AI

This name represents a friendly and accessible AI tool that offers emotional support through guided interactions, meditations, and well-being challenges. It is simple, memorable, and reflects the project’s purpose.

Optional domain: emotionalsupportspectra.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Spectra website is to introduce users to the benefits of emotional support through artificial intelligence. It will provide information about how Spectra works, showcase its main features (emotional chat, breathing exercises, games, guided meditations), and explain how users can benefit from using it in their daily lives.

Scenarios

  • How can Spectra help me manage stress or anxiety on a daily basis?
  • What features are available in the free version versus the premium version?

Color Schema

  • #00c6ff (Sky Blue): Used for buttons, headings, and icons to give a sense of calm and clarity.
  • #333333 (Dark Gray): Used for body text to ensure readability and accessibility.
  • #f0f8ff (Light Blue): Used for background to keep the interface light and soothing.

Typography

  • Heading font: Arial (sans-serif) – Clean and modern for headings.
  • Body font: Arial – Consistent and accessible throughout the text.
